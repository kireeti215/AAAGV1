Our Purpose

Why AAAG Exists

We believe every alumnus carries the power to light another's path. AAAG is the bridge between where we came from and where we're going.

🤝

Connect

Reunite alumni across all batches — from 1986 to today — into a living, active network.

💡

Share Knowledge

Share career experiences, professional journeys, and life lessons with the broader community.

🌱

Create Impact

Run mentorship programs, scholarship initiatives, and community-driven events for students.

🎓

Guide Juniors

Be the guide that we ourselves needed — helping current students find direction and opportunity.
