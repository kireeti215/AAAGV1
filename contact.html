Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the AAAG Alumni Directory? ▾

Visit the Alumni Directory page and click "Join Now". Fill in your details and submit. The committee will review and approve your profile within a few days.

When is the Maata-Baata session held? ▾

Maata-Baata sessions are held every Saturday from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM online. Join the WhatsApp group to receive session links and topic announcements.

Who can join AAAG? ▾

Any alumnus who studied at AP Residential School, Gandipalem — from the first batch of 1986 to the most recent — is welcome to join AAAG. Current students can also participate in Maata-Baata sessions.

Is there a membership fee? ▾

AAAG membership and the alumni directory are free. Participation in Maata-Baata sessions is also free. Voluntary contributions to the scholarship fund are always welcome.

How can I suggest a topic for Maata-Baata? ▾

Visit the Programs & Events page and use the "Suggest a Topic" section. You can also message the committee directly through the contact form above.

How do I volunteer for AAAG activities? ▾

Use the contact form above and select "Join as Volunteer" as the reason. Describe your area of interest or expertise and the committee will reach out to connect you with the right wing.
